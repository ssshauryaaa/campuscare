=========================================
CAMPUSCARE CODE OF CONDUCT
=========================================

1. RESPECT AND DIGNITY
All members of the campus community must be treated with respect. Harassment, discrimination, or bullying of any kind will not be tolerated.

2. SAFETY AND SECURITY
Do not prop open secure doors. Report any suspicious activities to campus security immediately.
Emergency contact: 555-0199

3. DIGITAL CONDUCT
Cyberbullying and unauthorized access to student or staff records are severe violations.
The administration logs all network activity for security purposes.

By accessing the CampusCare portal, you agree to abide by this Code of Conduct.
