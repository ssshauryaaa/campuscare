CAMPUSCARE STUDENT HANDBOOK 2025-2026
=========================================

1. ACADEMIC INTEGRITY
Students are expected to maintain the highest standards of academic honesty. Plagiarism, cheating, and unauthorized collaboration are strictly prohibited.

2. CAMPUS FACILITIES
The library is open from 8:00 AM to 8:00 PM on weekdays. Student ID cards must be presented upon entry.
The sports complex is available for all registered students. Proper attire is required.

3. IT POLICIES
Network access is a privilege. Do not share your CampusCare credentials with anyone.
Students attempting to bypass network filters or access unauthorized systems will face disciplinary action.

4. DRESS CODE
Students must adhere to the smart-casual dress code while on campus premises.

For any queries, contact the administration office.
